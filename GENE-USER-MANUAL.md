## üí∞ 2. CRITICAL: What to do when you Sell Art

**Your website does not automatically mark items as "Sold". You must do this.**

When someone buys a painting, here is what happens:
1.  **You get an email** from Stripe saying "Payment Received."
2.  **You must log in** to the website immediately to stop someone else from buying it.

### Step-by-Step: Marking an Item as "Sold"
1.  Log in to the Admin Panel.
2.  Click **"Artwork"** on the left menu.
3.  Click on the painting that was just sold.
4.  Find the switch that says **"Available"**.
5.  Click it so it turns **OFF** (Grey).
6.  Click the **"Publish"** button at the top.
7.  **Done!** It will now show as "Sold" on the website.

## üñºÔ∏è 3. Adding New Artwork

1.  Log in to the Admin Panel.
2.  Click **"Artwork"** on the left menu.
3.  Click the blue **"New Artwork"** button.
4.  Fill in the boxes:
    *   **Title:** The name of the piece.
    *   **Category:** Choose from the list (Clay, Drawings, etc.).
    *   **Description:** A short story about the piece.
    *   **Price:** Just the number (e.g., `50`).
    *   **Image:** Click to upload a photo from your computer.
    *   **Dimensions:** (Optional) e.g., "50cm x 30cm".
    *   **Weight:** (Optional) e.g., "2.5" (used for shipping calculations).
5.  Make sure **"Available"** is switched **ON** (Green/Blue).
6.  Click **"Publish"** at the top.

*Note: It takes about 1-2 minutes for the new artwork to appear on the live website.*

---

## üöö 4. Changing Shipping & Pickup Text

If you move house or want to change your delivery area:

1.  Click **"Site Settings"** on the left.
2.  Click **"General Settings"**.
3.  Scroll down to **"Shipping Rates"**.
4.  You can change:
    *   **Pickup Label:** (e.g., "Pickup from Earlville")
    *   **Local Delivery Text:** (e.g., "Delivery within 50km")
    *   **Prices:** The cost for Local, Standard, and Express shipping.
5.  Click **"Publish"** to save.

---

## üèñÔ∏è 5. Going on Holiday (Holiday Mode)

If you are going away and can't post items:

1.  Go to **"Site Settings"** -> **"General Settings"**.
2.  Scroll down to **"Holiday Mode"**.
3.  Switch **"Enable Holiday Mode"** to **ON**.
4.  (Optional) Enter the date you return.
5.  Click **"Publish"**.

**What happens?**
*   The "Buy" buttons disappear so no one can order.
*   A banner appears at the top saying you are away.
*   When you return, just come back here and switch it **OFF**.
